Information We Collect

Depending on the app or service you use, Ikehai may collect the following:

  • Contact details such as name, email address, or phone number.
  • Account and authentication data, including verification details used to sign in.
  • Content you submit, such as messages, notes, forms, documents, or images.
  • Usage information, such as app interactions, crash data, diagnostics, and device identifiers.
  • Technical data, such as IP address, browser type, operating system, and approximate region.
  • Permissions-based data, such as camera, photo library, notifications, or location access, when enabled by you and required by an app feature.

Some information is collected directly from you, some is collected automatically when you use a product, and some may be provided by third-party services that support the product.

How We Use Information

Ikehai may use personal information to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve apps and services.
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts.
  • Process requests, uploads, and user-submitted content.
  • Send service-related communications, such as verification codes, updates, and support responses.
  • Monitor performance, fix bugs, and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce applicable terms.

How Information May Be Shared

Ikehai may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers that help operate the apps and services, such as hosting, analytics, authentication, communications, storage, and infrastructure vendors.
  • When you direct us to share information through a product feature or integration.
  • When disclosure is reasonably necessary to comply with law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • To protect the rights, property, and safety of Ikehai, users, or others.
  • As part of a business transfer such as a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

Ikehai does not sell personal information for money. If a product later uses advertising or analytics features that qualify as sharing under certain privacy laws, this policy or a product-specific notice may be updated accordingly.

Security

Ikehai uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect personal information. No system is completely secure, and no transmission or storage method can be guaranteed to be fully secure.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live and the product you use, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or export of your personal information. You may also be able to object to or restrict certain processing.

You can also control some information through your device or browser settings, such as permissions for camera, photos, notifications, and location. To make a privacy request, use the contact details below.

Ikehai apps and services are not intended for children under 13, and Ikehai does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and address the issue.
