Privacy Policy

FieldTrack

Last updated: June 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the FieldTrack mobile application published by Ikehai. In some technical identifiers or internal UI copy, the app may also be referenced as FieldTech. The Android package identifier used for the app is com.d8ydev.fieldtech.

FieldTrack helps field technicians review assigned jobs, capture before-and-after photos, add notes, and complete job closeout steps from a mobile device.

Information We Collect

When you use FieldTrack, we may collect or process the following categories of information:

  • Authentication data, including your phone number and one-time passcode verification status.
  • Job-related data assigned to you, such as service details, property or worksite address information, status, and work notes.
  • Content you create in the app, including technician notes and before-and-after job photos.
  • Device and technical information needed to operate the app, such as app version, device type, error logs, and diagnostic information.
  • Local app storage data required to keep you signed in and manage in-progress uploads.

FieldTrack requests access to your camera and photo library so you can capture or attach job photos. The app does not ask for precise device location in order to provide its core functionality.

How We Use Information

  • Authenticate users and confirm technician access by phone-based verification.
  • Display assigned jobs and related work details.
  • Upload and store job photos and notes as part of work documentation.
  • Track job progress from assignment through completion.
  • Maintain app performance, troubleshoot issues, and protect the security of the service.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms or operational requirements.

How Information Is Shared

We may share information only as needed to operate FieldTrack and support the work it performs.

  • With communications providers used for one-time passcode verification.
  • With work-management and storage systems used to deliver assigned jobs, notes, and photo documentation.
  • With infrastructure and hosting providers that help us operate the app backend.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or a valid governmental request.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Ikehai, users, customers, or others.

We do not sell personal information for money.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide FieldTrack, maintain business records, support job documentation, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and meet legal obligations. Retention periods vary based on the type of information and operational needs.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or export of your personal information. You may also be able to control camera and photo permissions through your device settings.

To make a privacy request or ask a question about this policy, contact us using the email below.

Children's Privacy

FieldTrack is intended for business and field technician use and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
